FIGSY — AI SDR
Like hiring your best sales rep.
At a fraction of the cost.

FIGSY is a fully autonomous AI Sales Development Representative — not a template tool, not a mail merge. It thinks about each lead individually and runs the entire outreach cycle without you.

🤖 FIGSY in action

From lead to booked meeting — without lifting a finger

FIGSY receives each scored lead from your pipeline, reads their profile, company, and role, then crafts a personalised email written specifically for them. It sends, monitors for replies, follows up intelligently, handles objections, and books the meeting when they're ready. You just show up.

1

Lead arrives

FIGSY picks up every new scored lead from your pipeline automatically — no manual trigger needed.

2

Personalised email written

AI drafts a unique email per lead based on their role, company size, industry, and public signals. Not a template.

3

Sent from your domain

Emails go out from your address — looks and feels like it came from you. DMARC and SPF compliant.

4

Replies handled

FIGSY reads every reply, classifies intent (interested / not now / opt-out / OOO), and responds appropriately.

5

Follow-ups automated

If no reply, FIGSY follows up twice with fresh angles — not the same email repeated.

6

Meeting booked

Interested leads get a calendar invite. You receive a notification. Show up and close.

How it works
ICP to booked meeting
in four steps

The full Lead Gen + FIGSY flow — from defining who you want to talk to, all the way to a meeting in your calendar.

1

Define your ICP

Log in to your K.I.N.D portal and configure your Ideal Customer Profile — industry, title, company size, geography, tech stack, revenue range.

2

AI finds your leads

We pull matching contacts from Apollo's 250M+ database, deduplicate against your CRM, score each one 0–100, and run the POPIA consent check.

3

FIGSY runs outreach

FIGSY writes and sends a personalised email for every qualified lead, monitors replies, follows up, and handles the back-and-forth — automatically.

4

You close the deal

Interested leads land in your calendar as booked meetings. You see everything in the dashboard — and you only pay for leads actually delivered.
