How it works
ICP to booked meeting
in four steps

The full Lead Gen + FIGSY flow — from defining who you want to talk to, all the way to a meeting in your calendar.

Step 01

Define your ICP

Log into your K.I.N.D portal and set your Ideal Customer Profile — industry, title, company size, geography, tech stack.

Step 02

AI finds your leads

We pull matching contacts from Apollo's 250M+ database, deduplicate against your CRM, score each one 0–100, and run POPIA consent checks.

Step 03

FIGSY runs outreach

FIGSY writes a personalised email for every lead, sends it from your domain, monitors replies, follows up, and handles the back-and-forth.

Step 04

You close the deal

Interested leads land as booked meetings in your calendar. You see everything in the dashboard — and only pay for leads delivered.

FIGSY — AI SDR
Like hiring your best
sales rep. Without the salary.

FIGSY is a fully autonomous AI Sales Development Representative — not a template tool, not a mail merge. It thinks about each lead individually and runs the entire outreach cycle without you lifting a finger.

Step 01

Lead arrives

FIGSY picks up every new scored, consented lead from your pipeline — no manual trigger.

Step 02

Personalised email drafted

AI writes a unique email per lead based on their role, company, industry, and public signals. Never a template.

Step 03

Sent from your domain

Emails go out from your address — looks and feels like it came from you. DMARC & SPF compliant.

Step 04

Replies handled

FIGSY reads every reply, classifies intent (interested / not now / opt-out), and responds appropriately.

Step 05

Follow-ups automated

If no reply, FIGSY follows up twice with fresh angles — not the same email repeated.

Step 06

Meeting booked

Interested leads get a calendar invite. You receive a notification. Show up and close.
