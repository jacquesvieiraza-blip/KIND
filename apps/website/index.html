FIGSY — AI SDR
Your AI sales rep.
Never sleeps. Never quits.

FIGSY is a fully autonomous AI SDR — not a template tool. It reads each lead individually and runs the entire outreach cycle without you lifting a finger.

🤖 How FIGSY works

Lead to booked meeting — automatically

FIGSY picks up every scored lead, writes a unique personalised email, sends from your domain, reads the reply, follows up with fresh angles, and books the meeting when the lead is ready. You just show up.

1

Lead arrives

FIGSY picks up each new scored lead from your pipeline automatically.

2

Personalised email written

AI writes a unique email per lead — based on their role, company and industry. Not a template.

3

Sent from your domain

Emails go from your address. Looks like it came from you. DMARC & SPF compliant.

4

Replies handled

FIGSY reads every reply, classifies intent, and responds — or books the meeting.

5

Follow-ups automated

No reply? FIGSY follows up twice with fresh messaging angles — not the same email.

6

Meeting booked

Interested leads get a calendar invite. You get a notification. Show up and close.
