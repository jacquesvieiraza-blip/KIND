5. What Constitutes a Qualified Lead

A qualified lead is defined as a prospect who sends a positive reply to any email in the 3-sequence outreach campaign. A positive reply includes any response indicating interest, a request for more information, a request to schedule a call, or any other reply that is not an opt-out or negative response.

The following do not consume a credit:

  • No response to all 3 emails in the sequence
  • A negative reply ("not interested", "remove me", "no thank you", or similar)
  • An automated out-of-office reply
  • A click on the unsubscribe link

One credit is consumed per qualified lead, regardless of subsequent engagement. If a qualified lead later goes cold or does not convert to a meeting, the credit is not refunded.

K.I.N.D makes no guarantee regarding conversion rates, meeting bookings, or sales outcomes resulting from qualified leads.

8. Data, Privacy & Compliance

Data We Process

K.I.N.D processes personal data of your prospects (name, email, job title, company, LinkedIn URL) sourced from Apollo.io's database of 250M+ verified B2B contacts. This data is used solely to run outreach campaigns on your behalf.

Consent & Opt-Out

Every outreach email sent by K.I.N.D includes an unsubscribe link. Any prospect who clicks the unsubscribe link, or replies requesting removal, is immediately added to a permanent opt-out blocklist. Opted-out contacts are never contacted again by any K.I.N.D client.

POPIA (South Africa)

K.I.N.D operates in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). The 3-sequence outreach model constitutes a legitimate interest basis for initial contact. Opt-outs are honoured immediately and permanently.

GDPR (European Union & UK)

For clients targeting EU or UK-based prospects, outreach is conducted on a legitimate interest basis under GDPR Article 6(1)(f). Opt-outs are treated as withdrawal of consent and actioned immediately.

CAN-SPAM (United States)

All emails include a physical address, clear identification of the sender, and a functioning opt-out mechanism in compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act.

Data Retention

Prospect data is retained for the duration of your active subscription and for 90 days following cancellation. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting hello@get-kind.com.

Sub-processors

K.I.N.D uses the following sub-processors: Apollo.io (lead data), Supabase (database), Resend (email delivery), Anthropic (AI email writing), Paystack (payment processing), Railway (API hosting), Vercel (web hosting).
