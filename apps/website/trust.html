Data Sovereignty
Your data never leaves Africa.

K.I.N.D runs on Supabase's af-south-1 region — Cape Town, South Africa. Every byte stays on the continent.

🌍

af-south-1 Cape Town

All data is stored and processed in Supabase's Cape Town region. No cross-border transfers to US or EU data centres.

🏛️

In-country processing

Lead data, email logs, contact records and campaign results are all processed within South Africa's jurisdiction.

✅

No US/EU data residency issues

Your African business data doesn't trigger Schrems II concerns or cross-border POPIA transfer requirements.

POPIA Compliance
Legitimate interest. Transparent. Always opt-out.

Our outreach model is built on POPIA's B2B legitimate interest framework — the same basis used by every professional sales team in South Africa.

  • ⚖️

    B2B Legitimate Interest

    We contact business professionals in their professional capacity, about products relevant to their role. This is the established POPIA basis for B2B outreach — not spam.

  • 🪪

    Clear sender identity

    Every email identifies who is contacting the prospect, on whose behalf, and why it's relevant to them. No disguised or misleading sender information.

  • 🚫

    One-click opt-out

    Every single email includes a clear, functional unsubscribe link. One click. No confirmation screen. No re-entry required.

  • 📋

    Permanent blocklist

    Opt-outs are logged permanently to a blocklist. That contact is never emailed again — from any campaign, from any client on the platform.

Security
Enterprise-grade security, startup-friendly pricing.

Built on Supabase with row-level security, JWT auth, and zero stored passwords. Security isn't a feature — it's the foundation.

  • 🔐

    Row-Level Security (RLS)

    Every database query is scoped to the authenticated user. No tenant can access another tenant's data — enforced at the database level, not just application logic.

  • 🔒

    Encrypted at rest

    All data is encrypted at rest in Supabase. Campaign data, contact lists, and email logs are protected even if storage media were compromised.

  • 🌐

    HTTPS everywhere

    All connections — API, dashboard, email tracking — use TLS 1.2+. No HTTP fallback. HSTS enforced on all K.I.N.D domains.

  • 🗝️

    No stored passwords

    Authentication uses Supabase magic links and OAuth. We never store raw passwords. Even if our database were exfiltrated, no passwords could be extracted.

  • 🎫

    JWT API authentication

    All API calls are authenticated with short-lived JWTs. Tokens are scoped to the minimum required permissions and expire automatically.

  • 🛡️

    Supabase infrastructure

    Built on Supabase — SOC 2 compliant infrastructure with dedicated database instances, WAF, and DDoS protection.

Email Infrastructure
Deliverability is not an afterthought.

We use Resend with a custom sending domain, full email authentication, and a domain warming protocol designed for high deliverability in African markets.

  • 📨

    Resend infrastructure

    All outbound email routes through Resend's enterprise-grade sending infrastructure — the same platform trusted by the world's leading SaaS companies.

  • 🌐

    Custom send domain

    Emails are sent from send.get-kind.com — a dedicated sending domain separate from our main domain, protecting our root domain reputation.

  • ✅

    SPF / DKIM / DMARC

    Full email authentication configured. SPF authorises our sending IPs, DKIM signs every message, and DMARC enforces policy — preventing spoofing and improving inbox placement.

  • 🔥

    Domain warming protocol

    New campaigns start with low volume and ramp up gradually. This protects sender reputation and ensures strong deliverability from day one of your campaign.

Our limits
What we don't do.

Some boundaries are non-negotiable. We've hard-coded these into how the platform works — not just the terms of service.

✕ No scraping without consent

We do not scrape contact data without an established consent or legitimate interest framework. All contacts come from verified databases with proper data licensing.

✕ No spam lists

We do not buy, rent, or resell mass spam lists. Every lead list is sourced through Apollo's verified B2B database with ICP-specific filters applied.

✕ No cold calling without POPIA coverage

K.I.N.D is designed for email outreach under B2B legitimate interest. We do not support cold calling campaigns without the appropriate POPIA basis established.

✕ No data sold to third parties

Your lead data, campaign results, and contact lists are yours. We do not sell, share, or monetise your data to any third party — ever.

✕ No permanent data retention

We follow data minimisation principles. Campaign data is retained only as long as necessary for the service, and you can request deletion at any time.

✕ No hidden sub-processors

We maintain a transparent list of sub-processors (Supabase, Resend, Apollo). No new sub-processors are added without updating our privacy policy.

Our team is based in South Africa and understands the local regulatory landscape. We're happy to walk through our compliance model.
