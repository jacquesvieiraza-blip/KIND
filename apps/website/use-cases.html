Financial Services

Compliant leads for
regulated markets.

Financial services firms operate in the most compliance-sensitive prospecting environment in any industry. POPIA in South Africa, GDPR in Europe, and FSCA requirements mean that how you source and contact prospects matters as much as who you contact.

K.I.N.D builds consent workflows and opt-out management directly into every lead — so your team can prospect with confidence, not legal anxiety.

  • POPIA & GDPR consent captured per lead before delivery
  • Opt-out blocklist — shared, instant, permanent
  • Target CFOs, Finance Directors, and Treasury teams by seniority
  • Industry filters: banking, insurance, asset management, fintech
  • Audit trail on every lead interaction for compliance reporting
Who we target

K.I.N.D helps financial services firms reach:

  • CFOs & Finance Directors — enterprise & mid-market
  • Treasury & investment managers
  • Business owners & directors of SMEs
  • Wealth management & retirement fund decision-makers
  • Procurement and operations leads at regulated entities
