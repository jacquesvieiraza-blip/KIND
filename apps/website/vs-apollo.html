Why K.I.N.D wins for African businesses.

Apollo is a US-centric product retrofitted to global markets. K.I.N.D was built specifically for South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, and the rest of the continent.

01

Africa-first data and coverage

Apollo's database is dominated by US and European contacts. South African, Nigerian, and Kenyan business data is sparse and often outdated. K.I.N.D focuses on African markets with verified, current contacts.

Apollo: sparse SA data K.I.N.D: Africa-first
02

Pay-per-result pricing — from $20/month

Apollo charges $99–$499/month minimum with annual contracts. K.I.N.D starts at $20/month with no lock-in — and you only pay a credit when a prospect replies positively. No results, no spend.

Apollo: no POPIA framework K.I.N.D: POPIA built-in

Data sovereignty in Cape Town

Apollo stores and processes data on US servers. K.I.N.D runs on Supabase af-south-1 in Cape Town — your African lead data stays in Africa, meeting data sovereignty requirements without workarounds.

Apollo: US data centres K.I.N.D: Cape Town, af-south-1
05

AI email sequences (FIGSY)

Apollo's sequences are template-based and require manual setup. K.I.N.D includes FIGSY — a Claude-powered AI SDR that writes unique emails for every contact, handles replies, manages objections, and books meetings autonomously.

Apollo: basic template sequences K.I.N.D: FIGSY AI writes every email

No annual commitment

Apollo's meaningful plans require annual contracts. K.I.N.D is credit-based and pay-as-you-go — buy credits when you need them, stop when you don't. No 12-month commitment, no cancellation penalties.

Apollo: annual contracts required K.I.N.D: credits, cancel anytime
07

Local support in your timezone

Apollo support operates on US timezones. K.I.N.D is run from South Africa — support is SAST-aligned, understands the local market context, and responds in business hours that work for you.

Apollo: US timezone support K.I.N.D: SAST support, SA-based team

Pay only for qualified leads

Apollo charges whether your outreach works or not. K.I.N.D only consumes a credit when a prospect replies positively — you literally don't pay for leads that don't respond.

Apollo: flat monthly regardless of results K.I.N.D: pay only on positive reply
