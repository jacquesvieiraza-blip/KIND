7 reasons to switch
No team needed. No enterprise price tag.

Outreach was designed for large US enterprise sales organisations with dedicated RevOps teams and six-figure budgets. K.I.N.D was built for ambitious businesses that want results without the overhead.

01

No SDR team required

Outreach.io is a platform — it amplifies an existing SDR team. You still need people to write sequences, handle replies, and book meetings. K.I.N.D's FIGSY AI does all of that autonomously, start to finish.

Outreach: needs SDR team to operate K.I.N.D: FIGSY is your AI SDR
02

Starts at $20 — not $300

Outreach.io requires a minimum of 3 users at $100–$165 per user per month — that is $300–$495 minimum just to get started, plus an annual contract. K.I.N.D starts at $20/month with no minimum seats and no annual lock-in.

Outreach: $300–$495/mo minimum, annual K.I.N.D: from $20 / R380/mo, no contract
03

10 minutes, not weeks

Outreach implementation takes weeks: CRM integration, sequence configuration, team training, ICP definition — you are looking at a month before a single email goes out. K.I.N.D onboards in 10 minutes and sends your first outreach automatically.

Outreach: weeks of setup & training K.I.N.D: first leads in 10 minutes
04

POPIA compliant from day one

Outreach.io data centres are in the US. There is no POPIA framework, no African data sovereignty, and no consent workflow built for South African regulation. K.I.N.D is POPIA-native: opt-out on every email, permanent blocklist, consent records, af-south-1 data hosting.

Outreach: US data centres, no POPIA K.I.N.D: POPIA built-in, Cape Town hosted
05

Works globally — including Africa

Outreach.io is built for US and EU enterprise markets. African business data, contact coverage, and market context are not a priority. K.I.N.D was built for Africa first — and works just as well for US and UK clients who want Africa-grade compliance.

Outreach: US/EU enterprise focus K.I.N.D: Africa-first, works for US + UK
06

Pay only on results

Outreach charges a flat monthly fee regardless of whether any meetings get booked. K.I.N.D is credit-based — a credit is consumed only when a prospect replies positively. Zero results, zero cost beyond your initial credit bundle.

Outreach: flat monthly fee regardless K.I.N.D: pay only on positive reply
07

AI personalisation at scale

Outreach sequences are template-based. Personalisation is manual — someone has to edit each email or configure tokens. FIGSY uses Claude AI to write a unique, contextually aware email for every single contact, every time.

Outreach: templates + manual personalisation K.I.N.D: Claude AI writes every email uniquely
