Outreach was designed for large US enterprise sales organisations with dedicated RevOps teams and six-figure budgets. K.I.N.D was built for ambitious businesses that want results without the overhead.

01

No team needed to operate

Outreach.io is a platform — it amplifies an existing SDR team. You still need people to write sequences, handle replies, and book meetings. FIGSY is the SDR, not a tool for SDRs. It does all of that autonomously, start to finish.

Outreach: tool for SDRs K.I.N.D: FIGSY is the SDR
02

Pricing 15x cheaper

Outreach.io requires a minimum of 3 users at $100–$165 per user per month — that is $300–$495 minimum just to get started, plus an annual contract. K.I.N.D starts at $20/month with no minimum seats and no annual lock-in.

Outreach: $300/mo minimum K.I.N.D: from $20/mo
03

POPIA compliance built-in

Outreach.io data centres are in the US. There is no POPIA framework, no African data sovereignty, and no consent workflow built for South African regulation. K.I.N.D is POPIA-native: opt-out on every email, permanent blocklist, consent records, af-south-1 data hosting.

Outreach: no POPIA framework K.I.N.D: POPIA + GDPR + CAN-SPAM
04

Setup in 10 minutes

Outreach implementation takes weeks: CRM integration, sequence configuration, team training, ICP definition — you are looking at a month before a single email goes out. K.I.N.D onboards in 10 minutes and sends your first outreach automatically.

Outreach: weeks of onboarding K.I.N.D: first leads in 10 minutes
05

Africa-first data

Outreach.io has no African market presence, no African language support, and no African data infrastructure. K.I.N.D was built in South Africa, stores data in Cape Town (af-south-1), and is designed for SA, NG, KE, and GH markets.

Outreach: no African market coverage K.I.N.D: Africa-first infrastructure
06

Pay only on results

Outreach charges a flat monthly fee regardless of whether any meetings get booked. K.I.N.D is credit-based — a credit is consumed only when a prospect replies positively. Zero results, zero cost beyond your initial credit bundle.

Outreach: flat fee, results or not K.I.N.D: pay per qualified reply
07

No contract

Outreach requires an annual commitment — you are locked in from day one. K.I.N.D offers a free trial, monthly billing, and the ability to cancel anytime. No 12-month risk before you have proven the ROI.

Outreach: annual commitment required K.I.N.D: free trial, cancel anytime
