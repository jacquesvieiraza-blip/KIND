8 reasons to choose K.I.N.D
Salesloft is complex. K.I.N.D just works.

Salesloft is a premium enterprise sales engagement platform. K.I.N.D is the alternative for teams that want the results — pipeline, meetings, qualified leads — without the enterprise overhead, the contracts, or the training investment.

01

Free trial — no card required

Salesloft has no free trial. You commit before you see a single result. K.I.N.D offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required — first outreach live in minutes, cancel before it costs you anything.

Salesloft: no free trial K.I.N.D: 14-day free trial, no card
02

6× cheaper starting price

Salesloft costs $75–$125 per user per month with annual billing. For even a 2-person team, that is $1,800–$3,000 per year before you see ROI. K.I.N.D starts at $20/month with no minimum seats, no annual lock-in, and pay-only-on-results pricing.

Salesloft: $75–$125/user/mo, annual K.I.N.D: from $20 / R380/mo, no contract
03

No trained sales team required

Salesloft is designed to be operated by trained sales development reps. Sequences, cadences, and playbooks all need human configuration and oversight. K.I.N.D's FIGSY AI is the SDR — it sources leads, writes emails, handles replies, and books meetings on its own.

Salesloft: requires trained sales team K.I.N.D: FIGSY replaces the SDR role
04

No annual contract

Salesloft requires an annual contract on all meaningful plans. If it does not work for your business in month 2, you are still paying for 10 more months. K.I.N.D is month-to-month — cancel anytime, no penalties, no questions asked.

Salesloft: annual contract required K.I.N.D: cancel anytime, no contract
05

POPIA and African compliance

Salesloft has no POPIA compliance framework and hosts data in US/EU data centres. For South African and African businesses, this is a legal exposure. K.I.N.D is POPIA-native: opt-out on every email, permanent blocklist, consent records, and data hosted at af-south-1 in Cape Town.

Salesloft: no POPIA, US/EU hosting K.I.N.D: POPIA built-in, Cape Town hosted
06

AI-native, not template-based

Salesloft cadences are template-driven. Personalisation is done with variable tokens or manually by reps. FIGSY uses Claude AI to write a genuinely unique email for every contact — not a mail merge with a first name inserted.

Salesloft: template cadences, rep-operated K.I.N.D: Claude AI writes every email uniquely
07

Works for Africa, US, and UK

Salesloft is built for US and EU enterprise. African market data, local compliance, and African business context are not considerations in the product. K.I.N.D was built Africa-first and works just as effectively for US and UK clients who want Africa-grade data governance.

Salesloft: US/EU enterprise, no Africa focus K.I.N.D: Africa-first, works globally
08

Pay only for results

Salesloft's annual contract means you pay whether your pipeline grows or not. K.I.N.D is credit-based: a credit is consumed only when a prospect replies positively to outreach. If no one engages, you pay nothing beyond your initial bundle.

Salesloft: annual fee regardless of results K.I.N.D: credits consumed only on positive reply
