# FIGSY — Explainer Video Script & Storyboard
**Format:** 90-second animated explainer
**Tone:** Confident, direct, modern — not salesy
**Use this for:** HeyGen / Synthesia voiceover, screen-record of demo, or motion designer brief

---

## VOICEOVER SCRIPT (read straight through)

> Your sales team is spending hours every week finding prospects, writing emails, chasing replies.
> Most of it never turns into a meeting.
>
> There's a better way.
>
> Meet FIGSY — your AI sales agent from K.I.N.D.
>
> FIGSY starts by finding the right people. Using your Ideal Customer Profile, it searches a database of over 275 million professionals and identifies exactly who you should be talking to.
>
> Then it writes a personal email to each one. Not a template. FIGSY reads their profile — their role, their company, what they've been doing — and writes something that actually sounds human.
>
> It sends the email. If there's no reply, it follows up at the right time, with the right message. The moment someone responds, FIGSY stops and flags them to you.
>
> Opt-outs are handled automatically. POPIA and GDPR compliant, every time.
>
> And when a lead is ready to talk — the meeting lands in your calendar. You just show up.
>
> While your competitors are writing emails one by one, FIGSY is working 24/7 — finding leads, starting conversations, and filling your pipeline.
>
> K.I.N.D. Launching 31 May. Join the waitlist at kind.ai

**Total read time: ~85 seconds at a natural pace**

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### For HeyGen / Synthesia
- Use a professional male or female AI presenter, business casual
- Upload this script verbatim
- Set speaking pace to "natural" — not fast
- Background: use the animated HTML demo as a virtual background, or use their built-in dark tech template
- Add captions for social sharing

### For a motion designer (Fiverr brief)
- Style reference: Alta HQ Katie video (youtube.com/watch?v=WDXXTI9YU-w)
- Brand colours: #6366f1 (primary), #0f172a (background), white text
- Font: Inter or similar clean sans-serif
- Duration: 85–90 seconds
- Deliverable: MP4 1920×1080, with and without captions

### For screen-recording the HTML demo
- Open `figsy-demo.html` in Chrome full screen (F11)
- Record with Loom (free) or OBS
- Read the voiceover script live, or record voiceover separately and sync in CapCut/iMovie
- Export at 1080p
